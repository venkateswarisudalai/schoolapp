## Full Description (max 4000 chars)
Mayuri Kids is a simple and powerful school management app designed for small schools and preschools. It helps teachers, administrators, and parents stay connected and organized.

Features:
• Student Attendance — Mark daily attendance quickly with a simple tap interface
• Fee Management — Track fee payments, generate receipts, and send payment reminders
• Student Profiles — Maintain complete student records including contact details and parent information
• Admin Dashboard — Overview of school metrics, attendance reports, and fee analytics
• Parent Communication — Keep parents informed about attendance and fee status
• Secure Login — Role-based access for admins, teachers, and parents
• Cloud Sync — All data securely stored in the cloud, accessible from any device

Perfect for:
- Preschools and kindergartens
- Small private schools
- Coaching centers and tuition classes
- Daycare centers

Built with modern technology for fast, reliable performance on any Android device.
